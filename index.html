Core & Back Strengthening Routine

Heavy Session

Warm-up (5 min)

Cat-Cow
On hands and knees, alternate arching and rounding your spine slowly.
10 reps
Dead Bug
Lying on back, arms up, knees bent 90 degrees. Slowly extend opposite arm and leg toward the floor without letting your lower back arch off the ground, return, switch.
10 reps / side

Main Block A - Anterior core (rectus abdominis / "six-pack")

Hanging Leg Raises
Hang from a pull-up bar, raise straight (or bent, if starting out) legs to hip height or higher, control the descent.
4 x 8-12
Cable Crunches
Kneel facing a cable machine with rope attachment, crunch down pulling elbows toward knees, focus on curling the spine, not just bending at hips.
4 x 12-15
Weighted / Decline Sit-ups
Hold a plate on your chest, sit up fully.
3 x 12

Main Block B - Obliques (side definition)

Cable Woodchoppers
Stand side-on to a cable set high, pull diagonally down across your body to opposite hip, rotating through the core.
3 x 10-12 / side
Weighted Russian Twists
Sit with knees bent, lean back slightly, hold a plate or dumbbell, rotate side to side, feet can be lifted for difficulty.
3 x 16-20 total

Main Block C - Posterior chain / back

Back Extensions
On a hyperextension bench, lower torso down, raise back to straight line, add a plate to chest for difficulty.
3 x 12-15
Romanian Deadlifts
Hinge at hips, lower weight along legs keeping back flat, squeeze glutes to return.
3 x 8-10
Pallof Press
Stand side-on to a cable/band at chest height, press the handle straight out from your chest and hold, resisting the rotational pull.
3 x 10s hold x 8 / side

Finisher - Static hold (2-3 min)

Plank with Shoulder Taps
Hold a plank, alternately tap opposite shoulder with each hand without rotating hips.
3 x 30-40s

Daily Light Routine

Plank
Standard forearm plank, hips level, core braced.
2 x 45-60s
Bicycle Crunches
Lying down, alternate elbow to opposite knee in a pedaling motion, slow and controlled.
1 x 20 / side
Bird Dog
On hands and knees, extend opposite arm and leg, hold briefly, keep hips square.
1 x 10 / side
Side Plank
Forearm side plank, hips lifted and stacked.
1 x 30s / side
Superman Hold
Lying face down, lift arms and legs off the ground simultaneously, squeeze glutes and back.
1 x 20-30s
